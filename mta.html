Details Regarding Career Drive


Aptitude Test

The online aptitude test will be conducted online on Hacker Earth Portal. The test will be consisting of 45 questions. Each student will be given 40 minutes. There is no particular syllabus for the test, questions can be asked from any topic like numerical or verbal reasoning to check your ability as the name suggests. If More than 1 Student gains the Top marks then Fastest finger first Round would be conducted to choose Top 3 among the qualified students. In the Fastest Finger First round, Students Securing top positions will be asked verbal questions and the student answering correctly first will be decided a winner.


Mock Interview

A panel of 2 faculties will take your interview. Students will be tested on their technical aspects. There'll be profile and branch-specific questions. Students will be asked questions according to their year, adhering to the subjects they studied. Everyone will get a participation certificate from Microsoft Technical Community and the best candidate will be awarded with a goodie.


Ethical Hacking Workshop

In the session, you'll be taught about the introduction, future and the scope of ethical hacking, virus creation, execution, and prevention, installation of Kali on Virtual Machine. You'll get to learn how to hack and secure both wifi and wired networks, how to crack passwords. You'll be performing SQL Injection along with the instructor on your own systems. At the last, you'll end up gaining a lot of knowledge and confidence in ethical hacking. You all will be provided with software toolkits along with study material, cool laptop stickers and a certificate from Microsoft. here will be a quiz in the last of the workshop and the best performers will be awarded goodies.


Azure Bootcamp 2.0

The workshop will cover a lot of noteworthy topics that will enhance your skills in the fields like Web designing basics, Cloud fundamentals, Microsoft Azure, Domain Connections, Google listings, etc. You will get to know about how to host a website, activating azure services, file transfer protocols and a lot more. All the participants would be given free Microsoft Azure credits worth Rs 6500/- including cool laptop stickers and a certificate from Microsoft. There will be a quiz in the last of the workshop and the winners will be awarded goodies.


CV Building Session

The session will help you to make your CV look more presentable. All of your myths regarding CV building would be cleared. You'll get to know what the employers are looking for and what are the things that you've to avoid while building your CV. The session will consist of various topics like prioritizing the different CV sections, correct formatting of the CV and a proper structure for your CV. At the end of the workshop, you'll be having a presentable and updated CV of your own along with some cool laptop stickers and a certificate from Microsoft. There will be a quiz in the last of the workshop and the winners will be awarded goodies.


Group Discussion

The group discussion will help in the batches of 10-10 students. Each batch will get the time duration of 5-10 minutes to speak. Any general, technical or current affair topic can be given. The marking scheme will be explained on the spot. And causing interruptions and using foul language will result in marks deduction. Everyone would get a certificate from Microsoft and cool laptop stickers. Best speakers would be awarded goodies.
