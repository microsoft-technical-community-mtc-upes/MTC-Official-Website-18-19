About Us

You ask who are we ?
We are no one except a bunch of people who want to see you at the top of the world.

A Student Chapter based on the ideology Your Potential, Our Passion which believes in enhancing your skills while gaining industry experience and thus turning you into a competent personnel.

We're completely focused on skill enhancement, using aids like tech talks and events which in turn concentrates on increasing the aptitude of each and every student who comes to us. We are going to learn and educate together, as the best educators are those who never stop learning themselves. We believe that growth must be mutual, we grow as our members learn.

Microsoft our patron is going to interact with us actively and will be leading us with their expert guidance.

What We Do ?

Workshops

Discussions are going to be conducted on the topics that will bring you the updates on the latest techniques in the world and help you to keep up with this exponentially growing technology.

Tech Talks

Tech Talks will give view the knowledge you need and which is required in the fast growing world to stay in the race.

Tech events

How about if you can apply your gained knowledge and skills in workshops and tech talks in your own way? Now, it's possible!!! With the technical events of Microsoft Technical Community you can do it now.

Hackathons

With the state level Hackathons you will get a chance to compete and compare your skills with the top notch students of other universities and perceive where you stand.


MTC is a community which keeps you updated with the innovation happening on a frequent basis. Our aim is to provide students with the technical knowledge which the world needs in the current scenario, for which I believe that MTC is a humble beginning.


Sahil Taneja
(Founder)
